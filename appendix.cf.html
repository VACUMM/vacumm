3.2. Default CF variables and axesÂ¶

Here are the specifications of the variables and axes managed by the vacumm.data.cf module. They are used to search for and format variables.

New variables and axes can be registered by calling register_cf_variable(), register_cf_variables_from_cfg(), register_cf_axis() or register_cf_axes_from_cfg().
